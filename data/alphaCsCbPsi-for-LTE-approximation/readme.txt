
All files have

First column: T
Second column: alpha, cs^2, cb^2 or Psi

The maximum value of the temperature corresponds to the critical temperature

Different cases: label 1 is related to low-scale, 4d RG scale 0.5*pi*T,  label 2 is related to high-scale, 4d RG scale 2.0*pi*T 

4d method: 

alpha1.dat, cbsq1.dat, cssq1.dat, Psi1.dat		# low-scale 
alpha2.dat, cbsq2.dat, cssq2.dat, Psi2.dat		# high-scale 

3d method: 

alpha3d1.dat, cbsq3d1.dat, cssq3d1.dat, Psi3d1.dat	# low-scale 
alpha3d2.dat, cbsq3d2.dat, cssq3d2.dat, Psi3d2.dat	# high-scale
