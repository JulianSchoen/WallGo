solveBoltzmannEquations() → ndarray[source]

Solves Boltzmann equation for \(\delta f\), equation (32) of [LC22].

Equations are of the form

\[\mathcal{O}_{ijk,abc} \delta f_{abc} = \mathcal{S}_{ijk},\]

where letters from the middle of the alphabet denote points on the coordinate lattice \(\{\xi_i,p_{z,j},p_{\Vert,k}\}\), and letters from the beginning of the alphabet denote elements of the basis of spectral functions \(\{\bar{T}_a, \bar{T}_b, \tilde{T}_c\}\).

Returns:

delta_f -- The deviation from equilibrium, a rank 6 array, with shape (len(z), len(pz), len(pp), len(z), len(pz), len(pp)).

Return type:

array_like
